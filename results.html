GREATEST UPSET IN REINING HISTORY — 2025

French professional Arnaud Girinon, largely unknown to US crowds, piloted Flexing Guns N Town to a 232 to win the $500,000 championship — beating every NRHA Million Dollar Rider in the field by two or more points. The crowd erupted. Announcers called it the greatest upset in reining history.

2025
August 16 · South Point Arena · Las Vegas, NV
🏆
Million Dollar Champion

By Hollywoodstinseltown × Shesa Fashion Gun. 2019 palomino stallion. Qualified via 2024 NRHA European Derby Open Championship. Greatest upset in TRFAM history — European qualifier beating all US Million Dollar Riders.

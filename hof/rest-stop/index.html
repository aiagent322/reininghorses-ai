Rest Stop

1988 Futurity Reserve · 10 Performers · Dam of Best Stop · 2010
Rest Stop was a 1985 buckskin mare by Topsail Cody (HOF 1996) out of Villa Eddie. She earned over $56,000 in NRHA competition including a Reserve Championship at the 1988 NRHA Futurity with Doug Milholland (People HOF 2006) and the 1990 Non Pro Reserve World Championship. After retirement she produced 10 foals, all performers with $176,500+ in combined NRHA earnings. Her daughter Best Stop was inducted into the NRHA Hall of Fame in 2025 — Rest Stop is one of only a handful of mares whose offspring followed them into the Hall. She passed away in 2005.
Career Highlights
  • 1988 NRHA Futurity Open Reserve Champion with Doug Milholland · 1990 Non Pro Reserve World Champion — $56,000+ NRHA LTE; by Topsail Cody (HOF 1996)
  • Dam of Best Stop (HOF 2025) — 10 foals, all performers, $176,500+ combined NRHA earnings; one of only a handful of mares with offspring also in the NRHA HOF
  • Born 1985 · Owned by Marc and Tami Wagman, Wagman Ranch, Talala, Oklahoma · Passed away 2005
